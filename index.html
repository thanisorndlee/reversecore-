ABSTRACT

This project, "REVERSECORE," explores the implementation of AI-driven Reverse Logistics systems, specifically focusing on sustainable packaging return technologies. By integrating Smart Reverse Vending Machines (RVM) with digital membership platforms, the system aims to optimize the collection process and enhance consumer participation. Through the application of Agentic AI and Metaverse-ready supply chain simulations, the study demonstrates significant potential in reducing operational costs and supporting circular economy goals in the retail sector, providing a visionary framework for future green logistics.
